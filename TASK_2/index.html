About RCB

Royal Challengers Bangalore, or RCB, is one of the most loved teams in the IPL. Since its beginning in 2008, the team has been known for its exciting cricket, powerful batting line-up, and passionate fans. Based in Bangalore, they play their home games at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium. RCB has had some of the biggest names in cricket and continues to chase its first IPL title. With a mix of experienced stars and young talent, the team always aims to put on a thrilling show for fans every season.
